Signpost

\ ˈsīn-ˌpōst \

Signpost is a life-saving, life-sustaining initiative. Through a portfolio of digital technology tools, Signpost provides accurate, accessible and timely information that matters most to people affected by conflict and disaster. Signpost protects lives by helping people choose which route to take to safety and access emergency rescue.

Who we are

WHO WE ARE



We launched Signpost in 2015 in a partnership with Mercy Corps as refugees were coming into Greece with nothing but their cell phones. We built a pilot there and have since expanded to three Signpost locations or ‘instances:’ Refugee.Info across Europe, CuentaNos in El Salvador and Khabrona in Jordan. Each instance has a customized portfolio of digital products based on the local needs and cultures of the populations we serve. Our core protection principles and practices remain constant across all instances.

Our strategy is to launch instances in partnership with other organizations - like MercyCorps in Jordan or through IRC directly as we’ve done in Europe. Through a nimble global team, we will provide expertise to instances in their early design and maturation phases assuring safe monitoring and use of sites. We plan to sustainably exit instances by handing over the programs to local partners who can continue to monitor and improve the safety and functionality of the tools for local populations as their needs change over time.
